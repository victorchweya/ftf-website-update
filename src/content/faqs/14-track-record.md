question: "What is Farm to Feed's track record?"
category: general
number: 14
published: true
---

We have a strong track record of success in improving food security and promoting sustainable agriculture practices in Kenya. We work with thousands of smallholder and commercial farmers to increase their productivity and income, while also promoting a market for odd-looking produce that would have otherwise gone to waste. 

Since the launch in April 2020, our current run rate has been rescuing 100,000 vegetable portions per week. Moreover, we have rescued over 920,000kgs of food and therefore saved 1000 tons of CO2 equivalent.
