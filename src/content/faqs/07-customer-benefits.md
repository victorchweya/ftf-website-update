question: "How do I as a customer benefit from purchasing from Farm to Feed?"
number: 7
published: true
---

1. Quality assurance: You can be assured of the quality and freshness of the produce you receive. We have a rigorous 3-step quality control process to ensure that only the best products are sourced and delivered to you. 
2.  Product variety: We offer a wide range of products, including both grade 1 and grade 2 produce, as well as odd-looking produce at a discounted price. This provides you with a range of options to suit your budget and needs. 
3. Convenience and effective service: We have an e-commerce platform that makes it easy and convenient to purchase products from the comfort of your home or office. With prompt delivery to your doorstep and excellent customer service, working with Farm to Feed is a hassle-free and enjoyable experience. 
4. Impact measurement: We provide impact statements in partnership with the University of Wageningen, we have estimated our per KGs impact in terms of CO2 equivalent. We track how many KGs from your purchase have been rescued and how much CO2 has been saved. This is also great for your own market reputation.
