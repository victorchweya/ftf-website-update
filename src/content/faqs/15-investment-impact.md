Investing in Farm to Feed has the potential to make a significant impact on climate change, food security, and sustainable agriculture practices in Kenya. 
Farm to Feed works to reduce food loss, which has a devastating impact on the climate. Furthermore, by supporting our work with farmers, investors can help to increase their productivity and income, which in turn helps to improve access to food. 
Lastly, by promoting sustainable agriculture practices, we are helping to mitigate the effects of climate change and improve the health of the land.
