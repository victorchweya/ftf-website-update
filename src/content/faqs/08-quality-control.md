question: "How does Farm to Feed ensure quality of produce?"
category: buyers
number: 8
published: true
---

At Farm to Feed, we have a rigorous quality control process in place to ensure that only the best quality produce reaches our customers. We have three quality checks: at the supply hub, the warehouse, and at the point of dispatch, to ensure that the produce is of the highest quality. 

Additionally, our warehouse and sourcing team are HACCP certified to ensure that they are trained to identify and prevent potential hazards and contamination throughout the supply chain. With our quality control process, we guarantee that our customers receive only the freshest and highest quality produce.
