---
question: "As a farmer, how do I benefit from working with Farm to Feed?"
category: farmers
number: 10
published: true
---

1. Alternative to food waste: Working with us provides farmers with an alternative to throwing away their unsold products. We off-take the full harvest, including odd-looking produce that would have otherwise been used as animal feed or composted, helping to reduce food waste and ensuring that farmers can profit from their hard work. 
2.  Increased income: Farmers working with Farm to Feed can expect increased income. We aim to pay farmers for their produce within 72 hours of supply, ensuring timely access to earnings. This enables farmers to purchase inputs for the next planting cycles, increase productivity, and ensure continued success. 
3. Downstream impact: Working with Farm to Feed has downstream impacts for organisations that depend on farmers, such as irrigation companies and fertiliser suppliers. By providing a reliable market for farmers, we are also indirectly supporting the businesses that support farming, contributing to the growth of the agriculture sector in Kenya.
