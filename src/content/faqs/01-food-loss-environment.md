---
question: "What is the effect of food loss and waste on the environment?"
number: 1
published: true
---

Food loss and waste have negative environmental impacts, including wasting resources, exacerbating water scarcity, causing chemical pollution, and generating greenhouse gas emissions, particularly in agriculture. to put in pespective, food loss and waste account for 10% of global greenhouse gas emissions.  Reducing food loss and waste is critical for sustainable food production and a healthier planet.
